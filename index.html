Admissions

Everything you need to know about applying to WAIT.

Requirements

Entry Requirements

High School Diploma Programs

  • Alberta High School Diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum 60% average in required courses
  • English 30-1 or 30-2
  • Math 20-1 or 20-2 (program dependent)

International Students

  • Valid Study Permit required
  • IELTS 6.0 overall (or equivalent)
  • Official transcripts (translated to English)
  • Proof of financial support
